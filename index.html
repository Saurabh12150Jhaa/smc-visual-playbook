Iron Rules

9 rules carved in stone. Break any one = stop trading for the day.

1
NEVER trade against 15-min bias
Bullish bias = only CALLS. Bearish = only PUTS. This single rule eliminates 50% of losses.
2
NEVER enter a mitigated OB
If price already returned to the OB zone, the institution closed their position. OB is dead.
3
NEVER skip the Tradable OB Checklist
4 mandatory features + 1 confluence minimum. If any mandatory missing = SKIP.
4
NEVER move SL further from entry
Only closer. Moving SL away = adding risk to a losing trade = account destruction.
5
NEVER risk more than 2% daily
KILL SWITCH at 2%. Close all. Walk away. Weekly: 4%. Monthly: 8%.
6
NEVER trade dead zone 12:00-13:30
Low volume = unreliable BOS/CHoCH. OBs formed here have low mitigation probability.
7
ALWAYS wait for 5-min candle CLOSE
Before acting on BOS/CHoCH. A wick is not a breakout. Wait for body close.
8
ALWAYS journal every trade
OB type, BOS type, liquidity context, score, screenshots. No journal = no improvement.
9
ALWAYS pre-plan both scenarios
Map bullish AND bearish levels before open. Every trade is pre-planned. Zero FOMO.
